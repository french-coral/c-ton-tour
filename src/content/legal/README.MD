# Legal Documentation (C-Ton-Tour)

This folder contains all legal documents for the C-Ton-Tour web application.

These documents are intended to ensure compliance with applicable laws (including GDPR and French law) and to clearly define the rights and obligations of users and the service provider.

---

## 1. Overview of Documents

The legal system is composed of the following files:

### Terms of Use
Defines how users can access and use the Service, including accounts, behavior rules, liability, and intellectual property.

### Privacy Policy
Explains what personal data is collected, why it is collected, how it is stored, and user rights under GDPR and other applicable laws.

### Cookie Policy
Describes the use of cookies and similar technologies (authentication, sessions, preferences).

### Acceptable Use Policy
Defines prohibited behavior such as abuse, bots, scraping, harassment, and illegal activity.

### Legal Notice (Mentions Légales)
Required under French law. Identifies the publisher, hosting provider, and legal responsibilities.

### Open Source & Branding Notice
Explains GPL v3 licensing for source code and clarifies that branding (name, logo, UI) is not included in the open-source license.

### Cookie Banner
Contains the exact user-facing text and consent behavior for cookie handling.
