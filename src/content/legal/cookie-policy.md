# Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how C-Ton-Tour ("Service"), operated by Matteo CATEZ ("we", "us", "our"), uses cookies and similar technologies.

This policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, improve user experience, and maintain secure sessions.

We may also use similar technologies such as local storage or session storage.

---

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

We only use **essential cookies** required for the Service to function.

### 2.1 Essential cookies

These cookies are necessary for:

- User authentication and login
- Maintaining active sessions
- Protecting user accounts
- Ensuring secure access to the Service
- Storing basic user preferences (e.g. interface settings)

Without these cookies, the Service cannot function properly.

---

## 3. Cookies We Do NOT Use

We do not use:

- Advertising cookies
- Tracking cookies
- Behavioural profiling cookies
- Third-party marketing cookies
- Analytics cookies (e.g. Google Analytics, PostHog, etc.)

---

## 4. Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party services used by the Service may set cookies:

### 4.1 Supabase
Used for authentication and session management.

### 4.2 Vercel
May use technical cookies for hosting, security, and performance purposes.

These cookies are strictly necessary for the operation of the Service.

---

## 5. Legal Basis (GDPR)

Under the GDPR and ePrivacy Directive:

- Essential cookies do not require user consent
- They are used based on the legitimate interest of providing a functional and secure service

If we introduce non-essential cookies in the future, we will request your consent before using them.

---

## 6. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings.

Most browsers allow you to:

- View stored cookies
- Delete cookies
- Block cookies
- Set preferences for specific websites

Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent the Service from functioning correctly.

---

## 7. Data Stored in Cookies

Cookies used by the Service may store:

- Authentication tokens (session identifiers)
- Login state
- Basic interface preferences

We do not store sensitive personal data directly in cookies.
